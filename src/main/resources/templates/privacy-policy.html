Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. NattyPro.Life is a community built on trust — we collect only what we need to run the platform and never sell your data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

NattyPro.Life is an online forum and community platform dedicated to natural bodybuilding. We are operated independently and are committed to protecting the privacy of our members.

2. Information We Collect

When you register and use NattyPro.Life, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Username, email address, and encrypted password
  • Profile information: Bio, age (optional), avatar image, and social media handles you choose to share
  • Content you post: Forum posts, comments, and any other content you submit to the platform
  • Usage data: Pages visited, posts upvoted, threads followed, and general interaction with the platform

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content to other members
  • Send important account notifications (password resets, email confirmations)
  • Enforce our community guidelines and terms of service
  • Improve the platform based on usage patterns

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect payment information, track your activity outside of NattyPro.Life, or use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that profile you across the web.

5. How We Protect Your Data

All passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing — we cannot see your password. Your data is stored on secured servers. We use HTTPS to encrypt all data in transit between your browser and our servers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety of our members or the public
  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g. email delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements

7. Your Privacy Controls

You have control over your information. From your profile settings you can:

  • Choose whether to display your age and email address publicly
  • Update or remove your bio, avatar, and social media links at any time
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us

8. Cookies

NattyPro.Life uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

9. Minors

NattyPro.Life requires all users to be at least 15 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 15. If we become aware that a user is under 15, we will terminate their account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered members of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us using the Contact form on the NattyPro.Life homepage.
